About Us

The where, how, and why

Pogfish Asian was founded by 3 aspiring AI Students, who quit their course to follow their real dream: rolling fish.

The inspiration was there: we wanted to impress our beloved teacher Mr. Chris Porter, with a mix of cooking and coding.
The plan was simple: Max and Lucas do the cooking, Mathias does the testing. With this ingenious plan in mind, they started.

Segmentation faults in one hand & sushi in the other, they slowly gained traction in the lovely island of Malta, turning a small local business into a world-renowned restaurant, whose sushi is sought after by tourists and citizens alike.

Clients get to choose whether they want to eat their spectacular food inside, whilst seeing our talented chefs cook and roll fish. Or outside, onlooking the beautiful view offerred by the Valletta Waterfront.
